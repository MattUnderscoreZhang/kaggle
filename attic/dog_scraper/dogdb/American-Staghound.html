Overview
American Staghound is a type of hound used in hunting deer course. Although they have been bred for many years, the American hound is not currently recognized as a dog breed.

American hound is not a recognized breed, but a type of course Sighthound used for a variety of games. The best way to find out if it is a hound mix together American and Scottish greyhound greyhounds characteristics. They run dogs with physical characteristics similar to greyhounds, has long legs, deep chest, strong muscles and visual acuity.
Body type
The color pattern of this breed is also similar to those found in greyhounds and greyhound Scots. Another similarity with the other two races, is the layer that can be found in three different types, the "fluff" that resembles the Scottish Deerhound, "stain" that looks like the Greyhound and the "broken" (a mixture of "fluff" and "slick" layer). This breed is known for having great speed and occasionally incredible endurance. They range in height from 24-32 inches (61-77 cm) at the withers and weigh between 45 and 110 pounds (20-45 kg). Although females are generally slightly smaller than men, it is not uncommon for females to reach the same size as the larger males.
