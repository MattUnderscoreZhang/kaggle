Alias Kelpie ( Kelpie ), where the black fur is also known as Babu ( Barb ). Many people think it is a border collie and the Australian Dingge dogs (Dingo ) of mixed blood, but the literature suggests it is a British North Country Collie offspring, this work very hard sheepdog, like many other shepherd dog, in the late nineteenth Century imported into australia.
