Overview
Australian Terrier from Australia, after the Kaine Terrier, Dandie Dinmont Terrier, Irish Terrier, Manchester Terrier, Yorkshire Terrier, Skye terrier cross, improved from the. 1872 to 1876 years, this kind of dog is very early historical records. In 1868, the Australian Royal Queensland exhibition, the exhibition rough-coated Terrier, is the Australian Terrier early prototype. In 1885, has long been known as the Australian Terrier Show in Melbourne in 1889, the Australian Terrier Club was established in Melbourne in 1896, set a standard for the dogs, and then to the rest of the world to 1933, British Kennel Association recognized. In 1960 by the American Kennel Association approved and registered, this is the first time in 24 years the terrier, is also AKC pedigree registered 114th species.. Color
Acceptable colors include Blue and Tan, and Sandy or Red.

Blue and Tan - the blue may be a dark blue, steel blue, dark gray blue or silver blue. The tan markings are as rich as possible on the face, ears, underbody, lower legs and feet and around the vent. The richer the color and more clearly defined the better. The topknot is silver or a lighter shade than head color.

Sandy or Red - May be any shade of solid sandy or solid red, the clearer the color the better.
