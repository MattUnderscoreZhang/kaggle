Overview
The Beaglier like the Beagle, is a designer breed, It is the cross of the Beagle and Cavalier King Charles Spaniel, can be small to medium in its size. Like many dog breeds, they are very lively, gentle, stubborn, energetic and athletic. These small dogs are ever so popular in Australia.
Body type
Small to medium in size, the Beaglier typically has the shorter muzle of the Cavalier. with a weight from 4.5 kg to 11.6 kg and a height of around 30 to 40 cm.  They do, however, retain the Beagle's Scent Hound ability. Both breeds are avid hunters. Beaglier's have large, expressive eyes, are lively, and athletic.
Coat
The coat of the Beaglier may be of medium length, Smooth coat, which has long outer or guard hairs then a typical Beagle.
Color
The most common colors for Beagliers are black, brown, tricolor, and white.
Temperament
Beagliers are good-natured dogs that feature a calm disposition. This wonderful breed for kids is known to be playful, energetic, and affectionate around them. It is also shy towards other pets.They make good watchdogs and they will bark or notify their family as soon as they hear or sense something that doesn’t seem right.
Training
Like most small dogs, the Beaglier can be stubborn. Training will be difficult, which might not be suitable for a first time dog owner. Training should be accompanied with fairness, firmness, tenderness, consistency and patience.
