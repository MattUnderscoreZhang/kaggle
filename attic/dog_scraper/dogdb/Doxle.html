Overview
The Doxle is a medium cross breed. It is the cross of the Dachshund and Beagle. The Doxle has a long body in proportion to their legs. They have a long muzzle and tail. They have large, round eyes and large, floppy ears. A hybrid breed may have any combination of characteristics found in either parent breed. They are friendly and loving dogs with lots of energy. They make good family pets.
Body type
Height: 9-12 inches, Weight: 12-17 pounds
Coat
The Doxle’s coat is short and sleek.
Color
They are found in many colours. Most common colours for these dogs are black, black and tan, brown, red and white or a mix of these colours.
Temperament
The Doxle is intelligent and may be stubborn at times. Training is important. They are very friendly and will make a great family pet.  They are very loving and friendly dogs who love their family. They are very gentle and patient with children and love to play with them. They are also good with other pets and dogs in the house. They are alert dogs and can make good guard/watch dogs.
Training
They are intelligent dogs and would learn fast.
Activity
Daily walks or playing in the yard is sufficient exercise.
