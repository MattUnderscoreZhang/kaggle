Overview
Komondor has a very strong force and lovely shape. A large, muscular, skeletal development, coated white rope thick coat. Komondor year living in the outdoor, its white coat can be hidden in the herd, from predators, thick coat can also resist the bad weather. Komondor is actually a guard dog, and not the shepherd.
Body type
Komondor tall, muscular, plenty of bone and substance, the proportion of the body is slightly longer than the height at the withers. The body is covered with thick, coat of white cords.

Male Komodo shoulder height of about 27.5 inches or more high; female komondor shoulder height of about 25.5 inches or more, male Mongolian dog weighing 100 pounds or more; female komondor weighing 80 pounds or more mature, the komondor should have sufficient bone and muscle. Large size is important, but the type, quality, symmetry, is also very important action and consistency, there is no reason for size at the expense of any one of them. The proportion of the body is slightly longer than the height at the withers. Is less than the minimum height of defects.
Coat
The thick, protective coat is typical of this variety. The puppy coat is relatively soft, but it is close to cord-like curls. The young dog coat, or intermediate coat, there are many close to the skin of the short "rope", is not obvious, sometimes is a mission, looks like on the rope and a layer of hair. The adult coat has two layers, one layer of undercoat like the puppy coat that, soft, thick, woolly, a layer of rough, rugged, curly outer coat. Rough hair stuck in the soft undercoat. The coat is a lasting, strong cords, touch are felt-like. Adult komondor was completely large ears, like the same rope is covered with hair. Need to remember one thing, its length must satisfy the komondor is functional requirements of hair. The young dog if the hair is short, will not be as defect. Straight or smooth coat is a defect, to two years of age without a long cord coat is a disqualification. The head and leg hair is short and smooth disqualification.
Color
Color is white, but it was not always white. Sometimes, can appear young dog can be a small amount of cheese or light yellow color, but with the gradually mature, the color will be faded. Ideally, the skin should be grey, pink skin is acceptable, but not ideal. In addition to puppy appeared small amounts of cheese or light yellow color, any color other than white are disqualification.
Temperament
Reserved with strangers, love, focusing on the host family or take care of his sheep, selflessness, or sheep in the family encounter attack, will fight. Combined with the love of family and flock, and the advantages of the dedicated, dog can be become one of the best guardian, family or herd vigilance, brave and very loyal.
