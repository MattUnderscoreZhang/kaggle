Overview
The Majestic Tree Hound is a large size scenthound that originated in United States of America. This is a relatively new breed and was developed from well established dog breeds of Medieval France. It is the cross of the Black and Tan Coonhound and Bloodhound. The most common colors for Majestic Tree Hounds are black, black and tan, blue, brown, gray, red, and white. It stands between 25-30 inches and weighs about 75-110 lbs. A long and narrow head starts off this breed and leads to moderately length muzzle.  To keep it healthy, it will need to get exercise regularly. The Majestic Tree Hound commonly participates in endurance running and hunting.

Training
Intelligent breed. Love to work. Easily trained but needs consistency.  Otherwise an obedient and easy to train breed, the Majestic Tree Hound can be stubborn sometimes and would need an experienced trainer to be a true packleader of this dog. It is a sensitive breed that will respond to praise as well as reprimands. It should be trained in a firm yet gentle manner with lots of praise and a bit of correction.
