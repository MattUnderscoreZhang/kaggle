Overview
The Miniature Bull Terrier is strongly built and muscular, which presents a symmetrical appearance, with a determined and intelligent expression. The breed is active, with a sweet disposition and is sensitive to the discipline. The dogs should look masculine, bitches should look feminine.
Body type
Long, strong and deep, extending to the end of the muzzle, but not coarse. The facial profile oval, with a smooth surface. From the top of the head to the tip of the nose slightly downward arc formation. Flat forehead. From the tip of the nose to the eyes and the distance is far greater than the distance from the eye to the head. Chin deep, perfect. Color eyes sunken, appropriate as far as possible, the eyes show enthusiasm, determined and intelligent expression. The eye is very small, triangular in shape, the location is a bit skew. The ears are small, thin, very close, ears erect.

Hair is short and smooth, rough texture rich luster. Skin tight.
