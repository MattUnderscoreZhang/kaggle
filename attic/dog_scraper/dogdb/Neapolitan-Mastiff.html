Overview
This is an ancient species, rediscovered in Italy in the 1940 s, Neapolitan mastiff is a kind of bone mass, big, awesome varieties, breeding dogs as guard dogs and is the purpose of protection, protection of owners and property. It is characterized by sagging skin cover the whole body, drooping head has a lot of wrinkles, and a lot of wattle. The classic of this variety is it show the appearance of the beast, frightening head shape and attitude, and memorable. Because of its overall structure, its movement has the characteristics of the swing, clumsy, not gentle or flowery type.
Body type
Strong solid, heavy dog bone, shape and burly, rectangular in proportion. Body length of about 10% - 10% more than width.

26-31 inches height: male, female 24 to 29 inches. The average weight of the adult, male 150 pounds, females 110 pounds, but in general weight are larger than the number, as long as the proportion and the function is not affected, the greater the weight, the better.
Coat
Coat is short, dense, and the body is the same length and smoothness. MAO and no more than 1 inch long straight. No fringed edge.
Color
Pure grey (blue), black, reddish brown or dark brown coat, and the color dark or light color series. Above all sorts of color can be some of the spots. With spots, spots must be dark brown (in contrast to the spots). There may be a pure white mark appear in his chest, from the chin to the chest, throat area, under the body, the penis foreskin, the back of the hock, toes. In the back of the wrist joint may also have white hair.
Temperament
The breed is strong and loyal, but not aggressive nor prone to bite without cause. The Neapolitan Mastiff is an alert, intelligent, noble and majestic defender of person and property.
Gait
Neapolitan mastiff movements not graceful, but quite slow and clumsy. General gait has walk, trot, flew and walking. Stride length and elastic, and at the same time very powerful, especially from the drive to the front leg stretch after a long driving force. Of all the gait are shaking and swinging of the body. When the show care will not be penalised. Forelegs slightly pulling movement is normal. The head level or slightly higher than the back.
