Overview
Otterhound, is a kind of tall, rough-coated hound, magnificent head showing great strength and dignity, and the strong body and stride large movements so that he can work for a long time. It has an extremely sensitive to smell, curiosity, and can stick to the study of. The Otterhound can capture prey on land and in the water.
Body type
Is a large wire-haired terriers, strong and dignified. It is a strong body, run long distance is very suitable for work all day. Otter dog has a keen sense of smell, can easily track scents. They with thick hard hair, like the web as the sole, gallop between land and rivers and lakes.

The male dog shoulder height of about 68.6 cm, weight 52.16 kg; she is 61 cm, weight 36.29 kg. But not absolute, only about index. Otterhounds body slightly rectangular body slightly greater than height. Otterhounds bones strong, muscular, with strong legs.
Coat
Long hispid and densely fine, hard, and secretion of oily substances, can resist the water in the water immersion, and can keep warm fur. This makes otterhounds can work in all kinds of bad weather and terrain. Standard otterhounds were hand induction hair for hard surface and soft and elastic. The coat can be up to 15.2 cm.
Color
All colors can be accepted. But the nose and eye rims should be dark in color, such as the liver.
Temperament
They have a open expression and friendly. His temperament is affable, boisterous and even tempered. The Otterhound has a nose extremely sensitive, and the is inquisitive and persevering in research of odors.

Since the hunts of Otterhound on both land and water, is requires a combination of features that ie unique among hounds. These include a rough, double coat and substantial, feet webbed.

The Otterhound never must be penalized when shown in working conditions. Any deviation from the breed standard is to be penalized according to the degree of deviation.
Gait
Running light is moderate, smooth and can keep running thousands of meters. But walk and relax, idle at ordinary times a bit lame. Otters hunting dogs in the jog is a single track gait, and feet off the ground is not high.
