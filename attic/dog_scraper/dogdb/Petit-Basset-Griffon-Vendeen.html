Overview
Petit Basset Griffon Vendeen is a kind of scent hounds, alias: small Wondai legs long curly dog, developed to hunt small prey in the rough and difficult Fenfanding area. In order to function effectively, he needs to have some special characteristics. Petit Basset Griffon Vendeen has a good temper, which makes them popular, happy, friendly, outgoing, independent, wide interest. Always make people happy, without any aggression. In the early to get a nickname: happy family. As the dogs, they are strong, agile, suitable for daytime hunting, like bark, sounds wonderful.
Body type
Size: regardless of gender, height should be between 13~15 inches, the error is acceptable in within 0.5 inches. Shoulder height greater than 15.5 inches to be disqualified. Proportion: body length than height, the correct ratio is from breastbone to the distance of hip shoulder high to about 50%. Constitution: the skeleton fructify, coordination and overall ratio.
Coat
Coat is rough and long, not exaggerated, touch feel acerbity. With thick and short hair. Can't show filiform or wool coat. Eye foil by long, stretching out his eyebrows, but it can't cover your eyes. Ears have long hair. The long lips form the mustache and beard. Abundant hair on the tail. Whole appearance look casual and messy.
Color
White with lemon, orange, black, three different color such as color or gray stripes (combination).
Temperament
To fulfill his hunting heritage, the PBGV must be bold and vivacious in character, compact, tough and robust in construction. The breed has an alert outlook, a lively bearing and a good voice, which is used freely. Expression is alert, friendly and intelligent. They are happy, extroverted and independent, yet willing to please.
Gait
Actions seem to stretch and fast. Forequarters movement is straight forward. Leave (observation) from behind, the hind legs parallel to each other, and shows a strong driving force. As speed increases, due to the effect of gravity, front and rear foot claws (body centerline) collapsed inward. As you can see, he is a lively dog, you can hunt for a long time.
