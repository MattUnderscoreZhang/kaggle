Overview
Polish Lowland Sheepdog (also known as the Polski Owczarek Nizinny) medium-sized herding dog breed, Originated in sixteenth Century, initially for gunning, now usually as a companion dog, sheepdog. His herding and working ability is attributed to an intense desire to please and nature. It is very lively, but with restraint, intelligent, understanding, and have excellent memory. The medium, its size Hardy, adapt to city life, often need to comb hair and a lot of physical exercise, the general life of 13-15 years.
Body type
The strong skeleton has good balance. Adult male horse height measured at the withers is 18 - 20 inches 17-19 inches, female. Preferably not lower than the standard size, so for a strong working dog too weak. Its shape is rectangular instead of square. The ratio of height to length is 9 - 10, which is the height at the withers should equal to the length of 9/10.
Coat
Double coat. The whole body is covered with a long, thick, shaggy, thick coat, very straight. The undercoat is soft and dense. The most typical is the long hanging hair covers its eyes. A slight wave coat can be. Defect: curly hair, lack of undercoat and short hair. Poland Lowland Sheepdog must look natural -- does not allow pruning.
Color
Can be any coat color. The most common color is white with black, grey, or sand flecks, white and gray, or chocolate color. Most because of the genetic, newborn puppies on the coat color to deep in adulthood, in addition to the birth of the white puppy.
Temperament
Stability and confidence. From a very early age, it needs a dominant master, and coordinated training. Without these, it will be to show the dominant master. When not used as a shepherd or working dog, it will be like it looks noble, suitable for any lifestyle. It is very loyal, but some cold and suspicious of strangers. It is very smart and have the super memory capacity and high intellect. It is very active, can bear hardships and stand hard work, is very friendly to people and other dogs, especially to his master, and his family is very loyal and obedient, and for children in the family is very love, but in the protection work and brave.

Defect: nervous, timid, or behavior is very bad.
Gait
Gait is smooth movements. When viewed from the front, from the elbow to the pasterns should move parallel to the. Toe to nature. Defect: toes outward. It's the biggest source of precursor is derived from good rear angulation. When viewed from behind, the hind legs should be parallel to each other, and not too close.

Defect: rear action too close.
