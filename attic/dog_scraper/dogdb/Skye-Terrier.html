Overview
The Skye Terrier is one of style, elegance and dignity, while being agile and strong with a durable, bone and muscle hard material. Race is long, and low - the ideal length of the body and the height is 2 to 1, which is considered to be the correct ratio.

Profuse race layer falls straight down on both sides of the body on the oval ribs. The hair is well feathered on the head, and veils the forehead and eyes to serve as protection against weeds, brambles and serious encounters with other animals.

The head is held high. The long tail is hanging. The movement is seemingly effortless. Body, quarters and jaws strong.
Body type
Size -- dogs ideal height is 25.4 cm, the female is 24.1 cm, based on the height, the upper tail of a high 25.4 cm dog the breastbone to the rump should be 50.8 cm. Whether female or male, a slightly higher or lower is acceptable. Dog 22.9 cm or more short, bitches 21.6 cm or higher will be punished. Proportion -- the torso and the height of the ideal proportion is 2:1, which is considered the most appropriate. The Constitution -- strong, full of strength, excellent quality and not rough, strong bones stout.
Coat
Double coat, the undercoat is short, soft and woolly. The outer coat is hard, straight and flat, 14 cm long. The trunk on both sides of vertical drop hair, from first to last the formation of parting. The head hair can be slightly shorter, it covered the forehead and eyes, the formation of a moderate "beard and skirt". Long feathering on ears from the tip of ear and its lateral edges straight down, like a fringed kind around the ears, outlining the ear shape. The ends of the hair coat on neck is mixed together, tail well feathered.
Color
Acceptable coat colors include: black, blue, dark or light gray, silver platinum, fawn and cream; all with dark points. Any of these solid colors may have shades of the same color or lighter undercoat, as long as the nose and ears are black. A small white spot on the chest is acceptable.
Temperament
Typical working Terrier brave, good dig, patience, strong, agile. Brave, good temper, loyalty. It to acquaintances friendly, enthusiastic, reserved with strangers and suspicious.
Training
When walking, the legs move straight. The current into the front legs, forming a continuous line. The distance between two and two tarsometatarsal joint distance as long. When travel straight forward, hind legs is the main driving force, forelegs should move well forward, without too much lift. The whole movement can be described in this way: lively, effortlessly, showing a smooth picture.
