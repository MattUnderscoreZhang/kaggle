Overview
The Taco Terrier is not a purebred dog. It is a cross between the Chihuahua and the Toy Fox Terrier, whether its origins are known or unknown. The Taco Terrier is a small cross breed known for being affectionate, aggressive, friendly, loyal, and playful.They are active and rattling levelheaded and less prone to being timid and cowardly than the Chihuahua, but likewise less aggressive and hyper than the Toy dog Fox Terrier.The most common colors for Taco Terriers are black, black and tan, brindle, brown, red, and white. The life expectancy of Taco Terrier is 13 to 15 years.
Body type
The fully young Taco Terrier is weighs from 07 to 10 pounds and height between 8 and 10 inches.
Coat
Mixed breed coats come in many shapes, colors, textures, and thicknesses. These small puppies have short hair with no undercoat and are average in terms of shedding.
Color
The coat of the Taco Terrier can be tan, brown and white with the Fox Terrier markings.
Temperament
Well behaved, extremely loyal Taco terriers are very bold and too daring despite its size. With a terrier behavior it is a little great watchdog. However, it is also friendly, playful and affectionate being a companion of the human family.
Active / Training
It will need daily exercise to maintain its shape. Committed and active owners will enjoy performing fitness activities with this breed.
