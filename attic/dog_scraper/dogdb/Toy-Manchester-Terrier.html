Overview
The Toy Manchester Terrier in North America is a small legged dog, always with a short coat marked with tan, a long tail and ears, that upright. In general, the color and shape of the toy Manchester Terrier is the standard for the Manchester Terrier. However, the Toy Manchester Terrier can not exceed 12 lbs (5.4 kg) in weight, and, unlike most Manchester Terrier, the Toy Manchester Terrier, cropped ears are a disqualification. The ears should be wide at the base and pointed at the tip. Burning ears "hood" is a serious offense, which means that it is not desirable to raise a dog with ears, does not mean that the dog has no disadvantage as a companion.
