Overview
The Cairmal is a designer breed cross between the Cairn Terrier and Maltese. They are  cheerful, energetic, friendly, lively, loving, loyal, and sweet. The most common colors for Cairmals are black, gray, red, and white. Life span: 12 - 15 years.

Temperament
They are friendly, lively, affectionate, fun-loving and a happy dog. They can get along well with other dogs and even with children. They make a family pet and a charming humane companion.
Active / Training
It will need regular exercise to maintain its fitness. Trips to the dog park are a great idea.

The Cairmal is a smart and intelligent breed. They are very easy to train but are sometimes stubborn which often hinders them to learn.
