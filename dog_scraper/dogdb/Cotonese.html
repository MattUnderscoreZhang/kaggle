Overview
The Cotonese is a mix of a purebred Coton De Tulear and a purebred Maltese. Alert, cheerful, energetic, intelligent, loving, playful, responsive, and social. It is the cross of the Coton de Tuléar and Maltese. The most common colors for Cotoneses are cream and white. To keep it healthy, it will need to get exercise regularly. They are cute little fluffy dogs that are very loving and affectionate.
Body type
These dogs are small in size with an average height and weight of 8-12 inches and 6-15 pounds respectively.
Coat
As both parent breeds are white in color, the Cotonese are also primarily found in white color though some Cotonese may show cream or biscuit color markings.
Color
Cream, White
Temperament
This is a suitable breed for kids and is known to be playful, energetic, and affectionate around them. They like to be with their family most of the time and make good companion dogs. Energetic and intelligent, these dogs are alert and attentive also and their wariness of strangers makes them very good watch dogs. These dogs are happy and enthusiastic dogs that need to be part of the family. They are very social and will get along well with other dogs and pets. They are excellent dogs to have as companion and family dogs.
Training
The Cotonese dogs are very trainable due to their intelligence and eager to please nature. This breed will require fewer repetitions to obey specific instructions, pleasing owners who like to see quick results.
