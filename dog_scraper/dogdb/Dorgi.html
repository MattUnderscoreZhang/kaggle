Overview
The Dorgi is a small designer breed of dog that has become quite popular today. It is the cross of the Dachshund and Pembroke Welsh Corgi. They are unique hybrid dogs that are small in size and longer than it is taller. The most common colors for Dorgis are black, black and tan, and brown. They are energetic, loving and eager to please dogs. They are amusing, intelligent and human loving dogs that enjoy the company of the family. They are generally good with all the members of the family. They bark a lot and will need to be trained to bark only to alert.

Exercise
To keep it healthy, it will need to get exercise regularly. Known to be multi-talented, the Dorgi participates in a wide variety of activities such as guarding, herding, military work, racing, and sighting.
Training
They are intelligent dogs and are easy to train but having Dachshund willful traits, they may sometimes show stubborn and willful behavior. This can be dealt with firm and consistent training sessions that can be reinforced with love and food rewards.
