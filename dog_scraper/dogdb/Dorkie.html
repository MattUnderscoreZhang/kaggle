Overview
The Dorkie is not a purebred dog. It is a cross between the Dachshund and the Yorkshire Terrier. The Dorkie is a small cross breed known for being affectionate, cheerful, gentle, intelligent, loving, and playful. The most common colors for Dorkies are black, black and tan, golden, gray, and white. The average life span is between 13 – 15 years.
Body type
It is likely that a Dorkie will be long-bodied and usually a bit taller than a Dachshund; however, not as tall as a Yorkshire Terrier. Height: 5 - 9 inches, Weight: 5 - 12 lbs.
Coat
The Dorkie coat is either smooth & short or long & silky. The coat of the Dorkie is very soft with light wispy hairs.
Color
The coat of the Dachshund can be dapple, black & tan or gold & black in color.
Temperament
This breed is intelligent, active, confident and playful. They love to chase balls in the yard and make loyal companions to their owners. This breed can be a bit wary of strangers and young children.
Exercise
This breed is very smart and easy to train. They are great for first time dog owners. Some tend to be picky eaters and it may take awhile to find their preferred diet.
Health
Allergies: The Dorkie may have skin allergies.

Skin Health: The Dorkie may have mild flaking of the skin as a pup.
