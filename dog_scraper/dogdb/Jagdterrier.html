Overview
Jagdterrier is a type of working terrier, originating in Germany, used for hunting quarry both above and below ground. This breed of terrier is also called the German Hunt Terrier.
Body type
The chest is deep but not too broad. The ribs are well sprung and carried well back. The back is strong and level, not too short. Loin is well muscled, croup is flat. The body length is slightly greater than the height at the withers.
Coat
Both hair smooth, hard, or any texture in between, are acceptable, but the layer should be thick and abundant to activate brambles, moisture and cold.
Color
The main body color may be black, black and gray, or dark brown, brown, red, yellow or lighter colored markings found on eyebrows, chest, legs and anus. Light and dark colored masks are acceptable.
Temperament
Reliable, Courageous, Sociable, Intelligent, Strong Willed, Adaptable
Gait
Terriers were often required to run with the dogs, being there to bolt the quarry must go ashore. Therefore, its ride is more trot canter. This requirement is to Jagdterrier. They should have runaway power and the tendency to a gentle canter at a reasonable speed when released. During the trot, the passage must be free and parallel, with good reach and drive.
