Overview
A small but sturdy dog, powerful and energetic work in either black and tan or liver and tan, with a double layer weatherproof and a natural tail that is carried in a slight curve.
Body type
Body length, measured from the top of the withers to the tail configuration, is about an inch longer than the height at the withers. The ribs are well sprung and extending well back into the body to a short loin. The top line is firm and level.
Coat
Double dense, with a thin layer and a hard coat is short, dense and flat. The top layer forms a slight mane on the neck. The layer should not show through the top coat or mane make being outside the body.
