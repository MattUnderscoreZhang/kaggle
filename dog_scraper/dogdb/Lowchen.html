Overview
Lowchen is a small, bright and lively dog, originated from before the Renaissance Europe, as a companion dog, in order to win the ladies of the court, be pruned into the likeness of a little lion. The characteristics of this species is compact, shapely body; a relatively short, broad head and muzzle; and the gait pride, lively, special emphasis is placed on the clip into the lion's mane. These physical characteristics, combined with an outgoing and positive attitude, special style formed this dog.
Body type
The ideal shoulder height range is 12~14 inches, whether male or female, shoulder high above or below this standard, all belong to the defect, and is to be penalized to the extent of the deviation. Correct body proportions are not square, body length (from the point of breastbone to point of buttocks distance) and shoulder high ratio of approximately 11:10. Luo Qinquan is strong and sturdy in bone, but never coarse.
Coat
The untrimmed coat is long, relatively thick, moderately soft in texture. Showing mild or moderate wavy. Wiry, wool quality, curly or flat coat is wrong, and are to be penalized according to the extent of. No pruning or other coat is allowed. Puppies are often have soft coat. Pruning: pruning according to the lion shape, the following parts of the need to reduce, to retain only about 1/8 inch in length: ribs backward from the last section, including the hindquarters, retain the mane mane, covering the last rib. The hindquarters are clipped to the hocks. The front from the elbow down drop to the knee joint, and the distance from the ground to the hocks are the same, the limbs are forming a so-called "bracelet". The hair on the tail began to drop from the root of the tail to tail, half of the position, retain the end of the tail feathers. The feet hair clipped to tiptoe, wolf claw is removed. The unclipped areas must be completely natural and untrimmed. All areas of the unclipped, in addition to comb and brush, will not allow Mao Xiuping, cut, shape or altered in any way by mao. In addition, the standard way any pruning, or cut long hair is a disqualification.

Temperament
The Lowchen is alert, intelligent, and affectionate, with the overall qualities of a loving companion dog. It has a lively, outgoing, and inquisitive personality.
Gait
Lively, upstanding, with good reach and high head carriage. The legs are parallel in action.
