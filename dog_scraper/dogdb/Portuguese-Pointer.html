Overview
Portuguese Pointer is a medium sized dog, well balanced with a distinctive face "square", floppy ears, and a tail that is carried Hanging when the dog is standing and the level of the back or slightly above when the dog is moving. The Portuguese Pointer body is almost square. The coat is short, flat-lying. In the back, the coat is dense and somewhat rough to the touch. The Portuguese Pointer has a strong hunting instinct. In the field, the Portuguese Pointer is a tenacious hunter in all types of terrain and weather.
