Overview
Pyrenean Shepherd is a half-breed of dog native to the Pyrenees in southern France and northern Spain, bred since at least medieval times for livestock, especially sheep. Worked as a shepherd active along with the Great Pyrenees, another mountain dog, who acted as guardian of the flock.
Body type
Body is quite long and well supported, the back is short, the croup is short and oblique, side and withdrawn, ribs slightly rounded and extending well back. The chest is the development of medium and only reaches to the elbow.
