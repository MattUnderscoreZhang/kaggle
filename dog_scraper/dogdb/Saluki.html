Overview
An old hound breed is the Saluki, their ancestors can be traced back to 6000 BC 7000~. The Egyptians regard it as holy dog, called "Egypt's loyal dogs", used to hunt gazelle. The figure beauty, visual sensitivity, work. Slender body like spirit. Ear length, by Mao Guangliang. There is a variant in the ears, long legs and Onoue Mo. White, pale brown, red brown, dark brown, dark brown and white is mixed. The male dog body high 58~71 centimeters (23~28 inches), weighing 20~27 kg (45~60 lb); female canine size small.
Body type
In proportion, the Saluki is approximately square when measured from point of shoulder to point of buttocks and the top of the withers to the ground. The chest is deep, long and moderately narrow. The ribs are moderately sprung. The rear is quite wide, and the back is well muscled and slightly arched. The hip bones are wide apart. Emphasis well collected.
Coat
Soft and smooth with a silky texture, slight feather on the legs, feathers on the back of the thighs and sometimes with slight woolly feather on the thigh and shoulder.
Color
White, cream, fawn, golden, red, grizzle and tan, tricolor (white, black and tan) and black and tan.
Temperament
Smart, friendly, gentle on the child, can also become a loving companion dog. More cautious of strangers, easily trained, for a strict but gentle training.
Tail
Long, set on low and carried naturally in a curve, well feathered on the underside with long silky hair, not bushy.
