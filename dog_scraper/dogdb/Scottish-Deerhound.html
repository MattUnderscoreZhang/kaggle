Overview
Scottish Deerhound was praised as "God created the perfect life", excellent shape, character, exercise capacity are excellent, a keen sense of smell, can bring down the 100kg above the deer, but also to the owners are very faithful, are friends of human beings. Not aggressive, not too nervous, is a very good varieties.

In medieval Scotland chiefs to hunt deer, it is tall and muscular, rough fur can withstand the harsh climate. Once the nobility of Scotland hot dog, and become the Scotland royal dog, Earl rank below can't have this dog. When the shotgun and is widely used in the deer, the substantial reduction in the number of. Now in addition to hunting or good companion dogs.
Body type
General education is that of a Greyhound of larger size and bone. Chest deep rather than wide but not too narrow or slab-sided. Good chest circumference is indicative of great lung capacity. The loin well arched and tail drop. A straight back is not desirable, this formation is inadequate to work uphill, and very ugly.
Coat
The hair on the body, neck, and quarters should be harsh and strong about 3 or 4 inches long, the head, chest and belly much softer. There should be a slight fringe on the inside of the front and rear legs, but nothing approaching the "feather" of a Collie. A wool coat is bad. Some good strains have a mixture of silky coat with the disk that is preferred wool coat. The climate of the United States tends to produce the mixed layer. The ideal surface is a close fitting tattered coat thick, hard or brittle to the touch.
Color
Blue-gray dark gray, darker and lighter or brindles and yellows, fawns sandy red or red with black spots. A white chest, white toes and a slight white tip to stern are permissible but the less white the better, because it's a dog of a single color.
Temperament
The breed is obedient, friendly and docile. They are willing and easy to train. Scottish Deerhounds should never be suspicious, aggressive or nervous.
Health & Grooming
As shown in the standard, short, stagger, tough, clear is the ideal state. Scotland deerhounds do not need to spend too much energy to care, nail brush every day a thorough brushing is enough to keep it healthy, clean. Metal comb can be an easy job to comb down foreign body of dirt, mud soon get rid of, gently shake can be removed. Finger or toenail retain a certain length, if too long, activities will have scratch ground jangle; but also can not be too short, the length is not enough cannot provide enough friction (Scotland deerhound running process by friction as one). Although the lines do not need to decorate, but every week with the high shearing knife to remove dirty hair, skin is very necessary. At the same time, often gently prune tail hair is desirable, often check the teeth, ears, and remove tartar and wax, lest inflammation. Some Scotland deerhound susceptible to dirty door prostatitis and other diseases, so keep Wangmen surrounding clean is very important.
