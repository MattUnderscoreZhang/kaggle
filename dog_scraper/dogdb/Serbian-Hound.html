Overview
Serbian Hound often hunt in packs, hunt large prey, such as boar, deer, and other small prey such as rabbits. In Slovenia, the dogs are often used as hunting. Beautiful, quiet, elegant Serbian Hound are born hunters. Strong physique, especially muscular shoulders the dog became in the rugged mountain hunting ideal animal. Hear the dog barking very high from far away places can, which is conducive to the hunting. As with other regions, the local breeders have successfully maintained the characteristics of the dog.
Body type
The body is slightly longer than tall. The chest is deep and wide. The withers are slightly pronounced, and the back is long, strong and straight. The loin is muscular, and the croup is slightly sloping, broad and muscular. There is a slight tuck up.
Coat
Short, thick, abundant and shiny, the coat may be slightly longer on the back of the thighs and the underside of the tail.
Color
Black and Tan undercoat.
Temperament
Stubborn and lively, tireless, tracking the prey be full of go. The need for strict and moderate master.
Gait
Free and energetic at the trot.
