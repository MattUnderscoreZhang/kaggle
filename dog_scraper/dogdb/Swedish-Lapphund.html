Overview
Swedish Lapphund is a very old varieties, and has been identified as the most ancient Asian small sight hounds as long history. Previously, this dog is mainly used for grazing and defend Samos people reindeer are not carnivorous animal. Until the nineteen sixties, the Swedish Kennel Club through the strengthening of breeding, to improve their work ability, thus obtained the full value of its protection, especially in Scandinavia and Britain and other countries, this dog is increased day by day.
Body type
The body is compact and slightly longer than tall. The chest is deep to the elbow, and there is prominent forechest. The ribcage is long and oval, with well developed last ribs. The back is level, strong, muscular and springy. The loin is short and broad. The croup is proportionally long, broad and slightly sloping. The belly is slightly tucked up.
Coat
There is a profuse double coat, with the outer coat standing straight off the body, and a dense, finely curled undercoat. The coat is longer on the brisket, back of legs and tail and forms a ruff around the neck.

Temperament
Strong and brave, loyal alert, very wary of strangers, need strict training. Active, alert, full of feelings. The breed is very willing to work. Is very flexible, subject to high agility.
Gait
Light, springy and ground covering.
