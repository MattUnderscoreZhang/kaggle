Overview
The Treeing Walker is a well balanced symmetric, dog, elegant known for his ability to run and trees play a variety of different types of terrain.
Body type
The chest is deep rather than wide, giving lung space. Rib cage is well sprung and long, extending well back. The back is moderately long, muscular, level and strong. Loin short, broad and slightly arched. Fold-to moderate. Proportion of total (measured from point of shoulder to point of buttocks and from withers to ground) is square or slightly longer than tall.
Coat
Smooth, glossy and fine, yet dense enough for protection. A close, hard, hound coat.

Gait
